How it works

Set up in minutes

Three simple steps and your plants are monitored 24/7.

1
🔌
Plug in your sensor
Connect your ESP32 moisture sensor to power. The LED blinks to show it's ready to configure.
2
📶
Connect to WiFi
Join the Moist Setup hotspot from your phone and enter your home WiFi details at 192.168.4.1.
3
📱
Monitor from anywhere
Open the Moist app on any device. Watch your soil moisture update in real time — wherever you are.
Features

Everything your plants need

⚡
Live readings
Moisture updates every 10 seconds. See the exact percentage, raw ADC value, and status at a glance.
🔔
Push notifications
Get an alert on your phone the moment soil dips below your threshold — before your plant feels it.
🌿
Multiple plants
Add unlimited devices and switch between them with a tap. Each plant gets its own dashboard.
🌍
Works anywhere
Data syncs through Supabase. Check your plants while you're at work, travelling, or anywhere.
📊
History charts
View 24-hour, 7-day, and 30-day moisture trends with a beautiful D3 line chart.
🎯
Plant profiles
Choose from preset profiles — Succulent, Cactus, Tropical, Herb, Fern — or calibrate your own thresholds.
💧
Watering log
Mark when you water each plant and track how long it stays hydrated over time.
🔋
Battery mode
Enable deep sleep on your ESP32 to run on a small LiPo battery for weeks between charges.
