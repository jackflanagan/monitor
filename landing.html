How it works

Set up in minutes

Three steps and your plant is being monitored 24/7 from anywhere in the world.

01
🔌
Plug in your Moist sensor
Connect your frog-shaped ESP32 sensor to any USB power source and place it in your plant pot. The LED blinks green when it's ready to configure.
02
📶
Connect to your home WiFi
Join the Moist Setup hotspot from your phone and enter your home WiFi password at 192.168.4.1. Takes about 30 seconds.
03
📱
Monitor from anywhere
Open the Moist app on any device, anywhere in the world. Your plant's moisture updates every 10 seconds — get alerts before it dries out.

The sensor

Meet your new
plant friend

A cute frog-shaped sensor that sits in your plant pot and keeps watch 24/7. 3D printable, open-source hardware — or we'll send you one.

Built on the ESP32 microcontroller with a capacitive soil moisture probe, USB-C charging, and an LED status light right in the frog's forehead. You can print your own or join the waitlist for a pre-built one.
